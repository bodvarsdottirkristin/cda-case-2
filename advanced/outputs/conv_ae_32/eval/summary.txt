The Conv1D autoencoder successfully learned a compact representation of 60-second HR, EDA, and TEMP windows, reaching a final reconstruction loss of 0.0066. K-Means clustering on phase-level latent vectors selected k=2 with a silhouette score of 0.345, indicating moderate geometric structure in the latent space. However, cluster evaluation showed almost no alignment with experimental phase: Phase ARI was -0.002 and Phase NMI was 0.001. The phase-cluster crosstab showed nearly identical cluster distributions across pre-puzzle, puzzle, and post-puzzle phases. In contrast, the clusters aligned more strongly with cohort, with Cohort ARI = 0.220 and NMI = 0.351. This suggests that the learned representation captures cohort or acquisition-session effects more strongly than latent emotional states.
