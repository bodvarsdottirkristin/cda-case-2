Cluster driver analysis
========================

Goal
----
This analysis identifies which variables are most associated with cluster membership. It helps explain whether clusters are driven by experimental phase, role, cohort/session effects, individual differences, or questionnaire variables.

Interpretation guide
--------------------
For categorical variables, Cramér's V measures association with clusters.
For questionnaire variables, epsilon-squared estimates how strongly the variable differs across clusters.

If Phase has very weak association but Cohort or Individual has stronger association, clusters are likely driven by baseline/session/subject effects rather than emotional phases.
If questionnaire variables show only weak effects, then the clusters have limited emotional interpretability.
